Projects

Project 1

A project inspired by Claudia Fontes work of art called "El Problema del Caballo. Requirements: experimentation with leather and denim. Collection development and materialization of one look.

Project 2

NYX: THE GODDESS OF THE NIGHT
The "Nyx" skirt draws inspiration from the mysterious and captivating essence of the gothic subculture, deeply rooted in the enigmatic allure of the night. Named after Nyx, the ancient Greek goddess of the night, this skirt is a homage to the beauty, power, and elegance found in darkness and twilight. The design reflects the duality of gothic fashion- combining structured elements with fluid, romantic details.

Project 3

COERCION AND EVASION
This is the result of a collaboration between Altaroma and Accademia Del Lusso Roma, entitled "THE CLASH", where, in my own way, I decided to interpret the theme of a clash between our present and the future, creating this capsule collection. "COERCION AND EVASION" is born from the need to protest, to raise my voice against a world that forces us to behave in the same way.

Project 4

This collection showcases a range of ethnic wear garments that draw inspiration from the vibrant culture and traditional heritage of Jaipur. The objective of this project was to create a collection that authentically represents Jaipur and its people, capturing the essence of their textile technique.

Project 5

This fashion collection pays homage to the film's enchanting narrative, capturing its essence and inviting wearers to embark on their own transformative journey. With its blend of whimsical details, symbolic motifs, and evocative silhouettes, each garment serves as a tribute to the enduring magic of Spirited Away, inviting wearers to embrace their inner strength and courage as they navigate the challenges of life's own fantastical realms.

    About Me

    Hello! I’m Stella Alonso, a passionate fashion designer with a keen eye for detail and a love for creative expression. With years of experience in the fashion industry, I specialize in crafting unique and elegant garments that reflect my artistic vision and the latest trends. My design philosophy combines classic elegance with modern innovation, resulting in pieces that are both timeless and contemporary.

    Throughout my career, I’ve had the privilege of working with diverse clients and collaborating on various projects that have honed my skills and broadened my creative horizons. From conceptualizing designs to selecting fabrics and overseeing the final production, I am deeply involved in every step of the design process to ensure that each piece is crafted to perfection.

    My portfolio showcases a range of designs from haute couture to ready-to-wear, highlighting my versatility and commitment to excellence. Whether it’s a bespoke gown for a special occasion or a stylish outfit for everyday wear, I pour my heart and soul into every creation.

    Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I enjoy creating it. For inquiries or collaborations, feel free to get in touch through the contact section.

    "Fashion is the art of expressing one’s personality and creativity through clothing. It’s not just about following trends; it’s about setting them."

    - Stella Alonso
